At Your Service

Kavish Energy

We have a fuel station dedicated to fueling your vehicle just in case you consider traveling through the Nduluni- Kitise Road. You don't have to run out of fuel along this route.

Welding Service

We can weld for you. Many of our clients have had doors, grills and repairs done to them. If you have a construction going on or a repair that you need, Kavish Empire will do that to you.

Kavish Hardware

You can get cement and other hardware materials from our store. Investors and those who intend to erect buildings or carry on general construction work don't need to worry. Kavish Hardware is here for your needs. You purchase 3 bags of cement and above and we deliver it for you.

Pressure Carwash

Are you worried about your tarnished vehicle when using this route. We would be glad to clean it using our pressure carwash just to ensure that you travel confidently.

Transport Services

We have a lorry to cater for your transport needs ranging from construction materials among other goods. We also have a van for those who wish to commute between Nduluni and Makindu Town.

Entertainment

Rentals

Are you considering relocation to this place? Kavish Empire has classic rooms for rent. We ensure that our tenants have access to free clean water and electricity at an affordable price. We want to make you find a home away from home.
